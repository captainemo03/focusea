Privacy Policy

How Focusea handles user-entered maritime data, preferences and advertising choices.

Focusea is a maritime decision-support workspace. The current public site primarily stores demo workspace data, account form values, reports and preferences in the visitor's browser unless a future backend is connected.

Scope

This policy explains how Focusea handles information connected with maritime calculators, broker workflows, account forms, report history, advertising, contact requests and user-entered shipping content.

Information users may enter

Users may enter fixture terms, voyage estimates, laytime events, port information, cargo details, insurance quote inputs, account usernames, Gmail addresses and locally saved report notes. Users should avoid entering confidential, personal, financial, legal or commercially sensitive information unless they understand the risk of browser-local storage.

Account and sign-up data

The public static version of Focusea may simulate account creation locally in the browser. Username, Gmail address, password validation state, last-opened page and saved workspace data may be kept locally so users can continue where they left off. A future backend may introduce server-side accounts, in which case this policy should be updated before launch.

Local storage

Focusea may use localStorage to remember appearance settings, report history, local account details, quiz progress and workflow state. This helps users return to the last workspace page. Clearing browser data may delete these records.

Cookies, consent and analytics

Focusea may use essential browser storage for site functionality and may use third-party cookies or identifiers if advertising is served. Non-essential tracking, analytics or advertising features should be controlled by consent tools where legally required.

Advertising and third parties

Focusea includes Google AdSense publisher metadata and ad code for review and future monetization. If Google serves ads, Google may process advertising identifiers, cookies, device data and consent choices according to Google's advertising policies and the consent message shown to visitors where required.

External links and data sources

Focusea links to maritime authorities, academic resources, news sources, GitHub and third-party services. Those websites have their own privacy practices. Source links, market labels and live/simulated badges are provided to help users understand where information comes from.

Retention and deletion

Browser-local records remain until the user clears site data, resets the browser, changes device or removes saved items through Focusea controls where available. Public GitHub issues remain according to GitHub's own settings and policies.

Security

Users are responsible for protecting passwords, Gmail access and browser devices. If a password is stolen, reused, disclosed or compromised outside Focusea's control, site management is not responsible for resulting account misuse or data loss.

Children

Focusea is intended for maritime education and professional decision support. It is not designed for children to submit personal information.

User choices

Users can clear local browser data, avoid entering sensitive information, use privacy settings provided by the browser, manage advertising consent where available and contact Focusea through the contact page for privacy questions.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated as Focusea adds backend accounts, file uploads, AI document processing, analytics, advertising, live data integrations or paid services.

Contact

Privacy, product and security questions can be submitted through the contact page or the public GitHub issue tracker linked there.
