Advertising Policy

Ads must never change maritime calculations or risk outputs.

Focusea is prepared for Google AdSense review and future Auto Ads. Sponsored content, if displayed, is separated from decision-support outputs.

Google AdSense

Focusea uses publisher ID ca-pub-8225865281154040 and publishes an ads.txt file at the site root. Ad serving depends on Google approval, policy checks, consent requirements and available inventory.

Editorial independence

Advertising does not affect voyage estimates, demurrage calculations, port intelligence, insurance quote indications, learning content, risk scores or document outputs.

User experience

Ad placements should be clearly labelled and should not block navigation, hide content, imitate site controls, encourage invalid clicks or appear inside forms where they could confuse users.
