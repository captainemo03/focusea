About Focusea

A maritime command center for learning, calculations and traceable decisions.

Focusea connects broker workflows, voyage economics, laytime, demurrage, port intelligence, market monitoring, marine insurance and cargo stability learning in one practical workspace.

Mission

Focusea aims to make maritime work easier to understand, compare and explain. The product is designed for students, brokers, chartering teams, port users, marine insurance users and maritime professionals who need structured tools instead of scattered notes.

Transparency

Numbers are labelled as live, simulated, licensed-required, calculated or user-entered whenever practical. This helps users understand which outputs are educational and which require official or licensed verification.

Independent project

Focusea is an independent project and is not an official AIS provider, legal authority, port authority, class society, insurer or exchange data provider.
