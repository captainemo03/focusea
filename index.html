What is Focusea?

A maritime broker workspace for chartering, laytime and operational decisions.

Focusea brings the daily work of a maritime broker into one traceable command center: fixture review, voyage estimate, TCE, freight, bunker, port cost, laytime, demurrage, dispatch, claim documents, market news and port intelligence.

For brokers Parse offers, compare fixtures and prepare recap or counter emails.
For chartering teams Estimate voyage economics, exposure, deadlines and port risk before fixing.
For maritime training Practice laytime, demurrage, claims, stability and port operations in one place.

Data Source Transparency

Every market number is labelled as live, simulated, licensed required or user input.

Trust Layer

Focusea separates verified public data, demo values, licensed market indexes and user-entered assumptions. This keeps broker calculations honest and helps visitors understand which numbers are operational signals and which ones are placeholders until a paid or official data source is connected.

Live Maritime news and public web sources Displayed with source links and refresh status when available.
Simulated Demo traffic, port congestion and training values Used for education, UX demos and scenarios. Not sold as real market data.
Licensed required Baltic-style indexes and paid market feeds Shown as API-ready fields until a licensed provider is connected.
User input Fixture terms, SOF events, cargo details and assumptions Calculations depend on the values entered by the broker or student.
Current rule: Focusea does not claim Baltic, bunker or port-delay values are real-time unless the card explicitly says live and includes a current source.
